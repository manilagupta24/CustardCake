Custard cake
INGREDIANTS:
1 and 1/4 all purpose flour
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup custard powder
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup oil
1 cup milk
tutti frutti or dry friuts(optional)

PROCESS:
Take all the dry ingrediants(all purpose flour,powdered sugar,custard powder,baking powder and bakind soda) and seive in into a large bowl 
Mix well with a spoon
Add oil and milk(gradually) 
Mix/whisk it to make a smooth batter
Add tutti frutti or dry fruit
Grease the cake tin with butter or oil
Pour the batter in
Microwave for 2-5 mins at 100 power level
YOUR CAKE IS READY!!
